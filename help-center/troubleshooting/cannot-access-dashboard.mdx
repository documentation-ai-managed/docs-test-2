description: Steps to resolve login or access issues.
---

## Symptoms
- Login fails or dashboard doesn't load
- Receive 403 or 500 errors

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your credentials">
    Ensure username and password are correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser refresh">
    Clear cache and reload `https://app.test.io`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disable VPN/Firewall">
    Some security settings can block access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">If problems persist, email support@test.io.</Callout>
