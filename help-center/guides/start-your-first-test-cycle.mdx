## Overview

Launch a test cycle to begin receiving QA feedback.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a project">
    Log in and click "New Project" from your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define test requirements">
    Add app URLs, test plan, and instructions for testers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch test cycle">
    Click "Start Cycle"; testers will join and begin testing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="info">You can track results in real-time from your dashboard.</Callout>
